Turkey pot pie

Turkey pot pie recipe made with leftover turkey

Chunks of white and dark turkey meat are mixed with peas, carrots, and beans in a creamy sauce that screams comfort food. Using ready-made pastry dough and frozen vegetables cuts down on prep time.

These leftover turkey pot pies are so delicious, they don't last long!

Ingredients

  • Frozen veggies
  • Fresh veggies
  • Butter and flour
  • Seasonings
  • Broth
  • Milk
  • Turkey
  • Pie crust

Steps

  1. Cook the frozen vegetables and celery.
  2. Saute the onions in butter, then whisk in the flour and seasonings.
  3. Whisk in the broth and milk to thicken the sauce.
  4. Stir in the cooked vegetables and turkey.
  5. Pour the filling into the prepared pie crust.
  6. Cover with the remaining crust and bake.
